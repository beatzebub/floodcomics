THE LINE GAME
a drawing game for 2+ players
it's simple, fun and easy to play

HOW TO PLAY:
you need: some drawing utensils, some paper (post-its are ideal)
1) draw a random line, trace a copy of the line for each player.
2) each player must draw a face or character, incorporating the line.
3) compare your drawings.
4) repeat, take turns to draw the starting line.
